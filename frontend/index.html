How It Works

Three layers of fraud detection

Every 3 seconds, PhishShield runs your call through a multi-stage AI pipeline.

STEP 01
🎙️

Live Audio Capture

Your browser's MediaRecorder API silently captures 3-second audio chunks from the call. No recording is stored — chunks are analyzed and discarded immediately. WebRTC ensures low latency.

STEP 02
🧠

Dual AI Analysis

Whisper transcribes speech locally. Librosa extracts 98 voice biomarkers (MFCC, pitch, spectral features). A trained Random Forest + LSTM ensemble predicts if the voice is AI-generated or cloned.

STEP 03
⚠️

Instant Risk Alert

A weighted risk score (40% voice + 60% content) classifies the call as SAFE, SUSPICIOUS, HIGH RISK, or CRITICAL FRAUD, updating your dashboard gauge every 3 seconds with color-coded alerts.

AI Training Data

Trained on real-world datasets

PhishShield's models are trained on industry-standard audio datasets.

🔬

ASVspoof 2019

University of Edinburgh dataset containing genuine and AI-spoofed voices. Used to train the voice fraud classifier to detect deepfake audio.

😤

RAVDESS Emotional Speech

Actors speaking with different emotions. Used to detect artificial urgency, fear, and anger tactics commonly used by scam callers.

🎙️

Mozilla Common Voice

Millions of real human voice recordings used as the "safe call" baseline to teach the model what normal human speech looks like.
